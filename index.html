About

Let me introduce myself.

I am Mehran Mehri,
a mathematics enthusiast, Henkel's data scientist,
and the CEO & co-founder of Callisto Huts.

I'm mainly focused on designing machine learning algorithms for chaotic and non-deterministic systems. In simple words, my final goal is to build a machine which can predict the future in systems with high sensitivity on initial conditions. (e.g. financial markets, success chance of an economy or a company, path of a hurricane, and etc.). In this personal website, I try to share useful mathematical and technical insights so others can also benefit from them. if you are interested in cooperation with me or have any question, do not hesitate to contact me.
